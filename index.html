Seungjun Kim

Assistant Professor

KDI School of Public Policy and Management

My research examines the intersection of international political economy and foreign direct investment, with particular emphasis on government-business relationships, industrial policies, and geo-economic statecraft. My work contributes to understanding how these dynamics affect the liberal international order amid intensifying geo-economic competition.
